Kedarnath

Kedarnath is a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site nestled amidst
the scenic beauty of the Himalayas.

Ooty

Ooty is a picturesque hill station known for its scenic beauty,
tea plantations, and pleasant climate.

Dhanushkodi

Dhanushkodi is a ghost town at the tip of Rameswaram Island, known for its mystical charm
and breathtaking coastal views

Thirupathi

Tirupati is a popular pilgrimage, known for its ancient temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara and its vibrant spiritual atmosphere.

Isha

Isha Foundation, founded by Sadhguru, is a non-profit organization that promotes holistic well-being, yoga, and inner transformation

Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a captivating state known for its lush green landscapes, backwaters, pristine beaches, and rich cultural heritage.
